Exam Strategy
⏱️
Section order
In Verbal: Grammar → Vocab → Para Jumbles → RC. In LR: Coding-Decoding → Syllogisms → Blood Relations → Seating. Fastest topics first, bank marks early.
📓
Maintain an error log
Every wrong answer — write the question, correct answer, and WHY you got it wrong. Review your error log every 2 days. This single habit boosts accuracy by 15–20%.
🎯
No negative marking
TCS TQT typically has no negative marking in verbal and LR. NEVER leave a blank — use process of elimination and always mark something. Free marks on the table otherwise.
